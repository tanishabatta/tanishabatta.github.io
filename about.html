Why Cybersecurity
I naturally like to stay prepared and think ahead in any situation. I tend to anticipate potential challenges and consider worst case scenarios, which helps me approach problems with a proactive mindset. Instead of seeing this as overthinking, I have learned that it is actually a strength, especially in cybersecurity. It allows me to identify risks early, plan for different outcomes, and stay one step ahead. Whether I am working on a project or trying something new, I am always thinking about how things could fail and how to prevent that. This mindset keeps me engaged and makes cybersecurity a great fit for how I naturally approach problem solving.
