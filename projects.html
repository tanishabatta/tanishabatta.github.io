Enterprise Network Merger & Secure Architecture
Network Design · Compliance · Cloud Migration
🏆 WGU Excellence Award
Engineered a secure two-company network merger replacing end-of-life infrastructure with modern Cisco, Fortinet, and Sophos equipment while migrating on-premises servers to Microsoft Azure, delivering the full solution under a $50,000 budget.
Implemented Zero-Trust security controls including RBAC, MFA, network segmentation via VLANs, and a DMZ to enforce least privilege and reduce attack surface across the merged environment.
Mapped the architecture to PCI DSS and GLBA compliance requirements to protect cardholder and customer financial data across both organizations.
